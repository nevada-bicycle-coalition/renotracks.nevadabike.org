An app for recording your bicycle trips. Using the app helps the City of Reno better understand bike routes and improve transportation efficacy throughout the city and Washoe County.

Track Data

Use your phone's GPS to record your routes. City planners will get to know which routes cyclists prefer. RenoTracks will allows you to contact the right authorities to get things like potholes or obstructed bike lanes fixed. See all of Reno's tracks on the interactive map.

Development

RenoTracks is a joint project created during the 2013 Hack 4 Reno hackathon in order to develop a reliable and accurate method of collecting cycling data from Reno bicyclists.
